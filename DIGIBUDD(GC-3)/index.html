About Us

Got stuck in a situation? Want a buddy for advice? Your Digital Buddy is just a click away, trained through Machine Learning, it is customized only for you and will analyze your situation according to the data you provide based on which it will suggest 2-3 real life practical solutions, thus showing you that there's always a way out. Also, user can share inspirational stories/write blogs/post videos based of people in your locality who you think can inspire others,users can put on calming background sound in their blogs, also users can audio reply to any post, users will be given positivity points based on their interaction with the application, there will be customized video/podcasts/activities/book suggestions for the users.
